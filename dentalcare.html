Dental Care Services

Tooth Decay services

Cavities can develop when bacteria in our mouth break down food particles, especially sugars and starches. Regular brushing, flossing, and check-ups can help prevent tooth decay.

  • Professional teeth cleaning
  • Plaque and tartar removal
  • Polishing and whitening

Dental Fillings services

Dental fitting refers to the process of adjusting or modifying a dental appliance, such as dentures, braces, or a mouthguard, to ensure a proper fit and comfortable wear. A proper dental fitting is crucial to:

  • Tooth-colored fillings
  • Amalgam fillings
  • Composite fillings

Dental Crowns

A dental crown is a cap that is fitted over a tooth, either to protect it from damage or to restore its shape and appearance.

  • Porcelain crowns
  • Gold crowns
  • Ceramic crowns
