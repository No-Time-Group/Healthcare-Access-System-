Self-Treatment Following Doctor's Instructions

It's important to follow your doctor's instructions carefully when self-treating. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Take medications as prescribed, at the correct times and dosages.
  • Monitor your symptoms and report any changes to your doctor.
  • Avoid activities or substances that may interfere with your medication.
  • Keep emergency contact information handy in case of severe reactions.

For specific conditions or medications, consult with your healthcare provider for personalized advice.
